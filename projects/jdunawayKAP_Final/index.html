Who Am I?

Through obstacles and tribulations I have learned life is not always fair, it is not kind, nor is it understood. We as a society do not know what others are going through; instead of asking we walk away. Sometimes we choose to tell, but noone is really listening. Why do we judge or assume? Why can't we just ask or better yet listen? We all have a story, we all have struggles and all of us are going through something. I am a person in hopes to make a difference. I want everyone to know they are someone and someone is always here to listen. I write in hopes to help one person and I rap/sing in hopes to help millions.
