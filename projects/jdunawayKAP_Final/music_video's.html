No One Understands

"No One Understands" is a song about being bullied and no one understanding the effects of what it can do to oneself. No matter how bad the bullying would get, I knew I was never going to let that stop me. I was going to overcome! That is not always an option for all, but through my music I hope to make a difference.

Changes

"Changes" is about not always being okay, but knowing its okay to ask for help. There are always obstacles in our life that we have to overcome, its how we choose to overcome them that defines who we are.

Never be afraid to ask for help, someone is always there to listen!
